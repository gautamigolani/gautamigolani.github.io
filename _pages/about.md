Hello!

I graduated from the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department from <a href="https://www.eng.nus.edu.sg/ece/">National University of Singapore</a> (NUS), where I received an M.Sc. degree in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Controls & Automation. Here, I was fortunate to be supervised by <a href="https://www.eng.nus.edu.sg/ece/staff/ge-shuzhi-sam/">Prof. Sam Shuzhi Ge</a> and be a part of his research group. I worked on my master’s thesis under his advice at the Control & Simulation Lab, focused on developing new motion planning schemes for an omni-directional mobile manipulator.

Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Mechatronics Engineering with a minor in Robotics & Automation from <a href="https://www.manipaldubai.com/mud.html">Manipal Academy of Higher Education</a> (MAHE), Dubai. My undergraduate thesis was focused on the development of an autonomous robot done under the mentorship of <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Geu_kHAAAAAJ&hl=en">Prof. Royson Dsouza</a> at the Manipal Centre of Robotics Excellence Laboratory.

<b>Research Interests:</b>
<ul> 
  <li>Manipulators and mobile robots</li>
  <li>Design, control, and motion planning</li>
  <li>Interaction of robots with the environment</li>
</ul>

If you’re looking to get into robotics or interested in robotics in general, I’d love to hear from you. You can reach out to me <a href="mailto: gautamig@live.com">here</a>.
